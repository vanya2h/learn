## Cover Letter Draft

For the past two years I have been building RWA tokenization infrastructure from scratch at Evergon Labs — ERC-7208 on EVM, a proprietary data standard for tokenized real-world assets. I architected the investor panel, issuer whitelabel, REST APIs, and a public SDK for third-party integrators. The protocol reached $200M TVL. What Ondo Finance is doing is the same mission at a larger scale and with broader chain coverage — and the full-stack role on the Web team is exactly where I'd want to be.

My stack matches the role precisely. TypeScript and React are my primary tools; I've been shipping production Next.js and React applications for close to a decade. On the backend I work with Node.js, Hono.js, PostgreSQL, and REST API design, plus blockchain-specific tooling: wagmi, viem, ethers.js, ponder.sh for indexing. At Rarible I integrated five blockchain ecosystems — Ethereum, Solana, Tezos, Aptos, Flow — into a unified marketplace codebase that scaled to 100K daily active users and 60+ third-party SDK integrators. The multi-chain surface Ondo operates across (Ethereum, Solana, Sui, Aptos, Arbitrum) is terrain I know well.

AI-native engineering is how I work, not an add-on. I use coding agents, IDE copilots, and automated verification loops as a core part of my development process — it's part of why I've been able to build full systems (client app, backend, SDK, indexer) as a small team.

Ondo is the most credible team in RWA infrastructure right now. I'd like to bring the same ground-up build experience from Evergon to the problems Ondo is solving at scale.

Ivan K.
